### Vision Statement
To create a reliable, scalable, and SLA-driven mental health service delivery platform that ensures timely crisis response, seamless counselling operations, and sustainable counsellor support through enterprise-grade ITSM workflows.

---

### Product Purpose
The platform exists to:
- Bring structure and predictability to counselling operations
- Replace manual escalation with automated workflows
- Ensure accountability through SLAs and ownership
- Protect both users and counsellors through reliable service delivery

---

### Target Users & Stakeholders
- End Users (patients, students, employees)
- Counsellors and therapists
- HR teams managing counsellor wellbeing
- IT and platform support teams
- Operations and leadership stakeholders

---

### Product Goals

#### Primary Goals
1. Guarantee time-bound response for crisis and counselling services
2. Eliminate dependency on manual escalation during emergencies
3. Provide operational visibility through metrics and SLAs
4. Support counsellor wellbeing via structured HR workflows
5. Increase trust through consistent and predictable service delivery

#### Secondary Goals
- Reduce repeat incidents
- Improve first-contact resolution
- Enable controlled and low-risk change deployments

---

### Scope Definition

#### In Scope
- Incident, Problem, and Change Management
- Service Requests and Service Catalog
- SLA definition and escalation logic
- HRSD for counsellor support
- Knowledge management
- UAT and go-live validation

#### Out of Scope
- Clinical diagnosis or treatment methodologies
- AI-based therapy or automation
- Marketing, growth, or acquisition features
- Direct counselling content creation

This ensures the product remains operationally focused and enterprise-ready.

---

### Product Principles
1. Human safety and wellbeing take priority over speed
2. Automation is preferred over manual intervention
3. SLAs must reflect real-world impact, not technical convenience
4. Every service must have a clearly defined owner
5. Preventing recurrence is as important as resolving incidents

---

### Success Metrics

| Metric | Business Outcome |
|------|------------------|
| Crisis SLA adherence | User safety and trust |
| Incident resolution time | Service reliability |
| Repeat incident rate | System stability |
| Counsellor HR case resolution | Workforce sustainability |
| User satisfaction | Confidence in service delivery |

Metrics are selected based on their ability to reduce risk and improve reliability.

---

### Long-Term Vision
The platform will serve as a foundational service layer that enables mental health organizations to scale responsibly, ensuring that growth does not compromise safety, quality, or employee wellbeing.

---

### Summary
This product vision positions mental health counselling as a critical enterprise service. By applying ITSM principles, the platform enables reliable operations, accountable ownership, and sustainable growth.
